About Us

Learn More About Us

  • Innores International, an international medical research community is, mainly focuses to perform interdisciplinary & basic research work and to provide scientific training for research among medical students and young doctors from all around the world through its Community and Study group.
  • Additionally, we work independently from any external influence, as a non-profit organization contributing to the evolution of scientific community.
  • Apart of research and educational purposes, one of our goals is to create a supportive community for medical students and simultaneously to establish strong friendships and collaboration, by exchanging details of language and culture among our members.
  • All our work, scientific and so, is held in English and all our members, students and mentors, must be comfortable with the use and understanding of English language.
  • Our organization is very well-structured, comprised of 3 main groups of committees (Group A, Group B and Group C), each of which has at least 2 members that are responsible for the smooth running of his/her committee.
  • They are the representatives of the Coordinator Committee and they are in constant communication with the other representatives for updates of the research work at the time.
  • Coordinators can be changed after the completion of one publication, which occurs every 3 months.

GROUP A

committees are related exclusively with the research process. These committees are:

  • Research question selection committee.
  • Data collection committee.
  • Data and Statistical analysis committee.
  • Final research paper draft and peer review committee.
  • Publishing committee.

GROUP B

committees are related with actions that are necessary for the legalization and promotion of our research. These committees are:

  • Guidance and mentoring committee with Professors.
  • Legal and ethics approval committee.
  • Technical committee.
  • Group A and B committees belong to both Innores study group and community.

GROUP C

committees are the ones that are only part of the Innores international community and not the study group.

Here includes the groups that are not related with the research work, but with the scouting of new members and extracurricular activities of our organization, such as collaboration with other communities, organizing conferences and getting familiar with other cultures. These are:

  • Social media committee.
  • Audit and finance committee.
  • Scouting new members and country wise Chapters committee.
  • Fun and cultural group.
